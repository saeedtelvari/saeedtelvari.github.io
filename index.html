  • "PhD Candidate"

    Welcome to my academic journey! I'm a PhD candidate at Heriot-watt University specializing in reservoir simulation and CO2 storage modeling. With expertise in Eclipse and MRST, I focus on developing advanced simulation techniques for CCUS applications and depleted gas reservoir characterization.

  • "CO2 Storage Research"

    My PhD research focuses on developing Vertical Equilibrium Models for simulating CO2 storage in depleted gas reservoirs. I work on advanced reservoir characterization, upscaling methods, and numerical simulation techniques for CCUS applications.

  • "Reservoir Simulation"

    Expert in Eclipse and MRST (Matlab Reservoir Simulation Toolbox) with extensive experience in fractured reservoir modeling and numerical simulation. I teach Eclipse simulation techniques and have developed advanced upscaling modules for complex reservoir systems.

  • "CCUS & Geothermal"

    Focused on Carbon Capture, Utilization and Storage (CCUS) technologies and geothermal energy systems. My research contributes to sustainable energy solutions through advanced reservoir engineering techniques and subsurface characterization methods.

  • "Porous Media Analysis"

    Digital rock analysis and pore-scale modeling for reservoir characterization. Expertise in image processing techniques for fractured rock analysis and advanced methods for understanding subsurface flow behavior.
